About Mehran University of Jamshoro

Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET) is a public university located in Jamshoro, Sindh, Pakistan. Established in 1963, it is one of the oldest engineering universities in Pakistan.

MUET offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines including engineering, architecture, and natural sciences. The university has a strong focus on research and innovation, and it collaborates with national and international institutions.

With its state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, MUET aims to provide quality education and produce skilled professionals who can contribute to the development of society and industry.

Research at Mehran University of Jamshoro

Mehran University of Engineering & Technology (MUET) is committed to raising the research quality by employing PhDs in conventional fields as well as in emerging areas of Engineering, Science, and Technology. In this context, MUET is fortunate to have a wealth of high-quality faculty members, working on innovative ideas and publishing research papers in impact factor journals, in addition to producing quality PhDs every year in Engineering and Technology. As a research-oriented university, we now have a substantial population of postgraduate students.
